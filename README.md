## ğŸ“ **Usage**

### **Recording a Transaction**

1. From the **Home** or **Dashboard**, click **Borrow** or **Lend**.
2. A modal will appear. Fill in the required details:

   * Asset Type (Utility, Cash, Gold, Silver, Loan)
   * Amount
   * Borrowed From / Lent To
   * Deadline
   * Interest Rate
   * Category (Emergency, Personal, Investment, Loan)
3. Click **Submit**. The transaction appears instantly on the Dashboard and Calendar.

### **Marking a Transaction as Returned**

1. On the Dashboard, find active transactions under **Transactions**.
2. Click the green checkmark (âœ”) next to the transaction.
3. The entry moves to **History**, where its status (Early, Late, On-Time) is automatically calculated based on the repayment date vs deadline.
